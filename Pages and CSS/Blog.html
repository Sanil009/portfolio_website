Effects of Technology in daily life

Sanil Shrestha | May 1, 2019

Technology has affected mankind drastically. There are both positive and negative impact of technology in our daily life. Technology has simplified the access to many necessary tools people need in education, industry, medicine, communication, transportation etc. But at the same time its development has complicated life in a number of different ways. Daily life of people is completely different than it was just a decade ago. It is much more simplier now and being more simplier with the never-ending innovations of new technology. We use technology to travel, to communicate, to learn, to do business and to live in comfort. There are technologies like vacuum cleaner and washing machines for cleaning purpose, TV, mobile phones for communication and entertainment purpose and biggest of all internet you can find anything there. Nowadays, people cannot live without technologies such as televisions, mobile phones, laptops, computers etc. These technologies have taken an essential part in peopleâ€™s daily lives and being without them would be unimaginable for many of us. Today's technology is changing and improving at the highest level of development. It is constantly progressing through innovations and changes. The evolution of technology has dramatically changed society. Everything has its own advantages and disadvantages and so does the technology. It has both positive and negative impact on our life.



Positive Effects of Technology

Technology has remarkably improved our quality of life. Life has been so much easier beacuse of technology. You can talk with people, your relatives across the globe. One of the technology that has made biggest impact is internet. Nowadays we can't even imagine life without internet, can we? You can read news, know what's happenning around the world with the help of a gadget that can fit in your palm. You don't need to search mall to mall, walk in pollution and heat, make a time from you busy schedule for shopping. You can do shopping in just a minutes on a click and your order will be on your door step. With the introduction of e-payment systems, people can easily purchase anything online without leaving the comfort of their homes. Online shopping, online foods, basically e-commerce has made a big impact on businesses. There is not a single aspect of life where technology has not been used. Most banks now offer online banking facilities. Millions make use of this service daily to manage their finances. Time is money, so we must have a fast and efficient mode of transport. It has become a basic need of every society. There has been an extreme rise of electric and hybrid vehicles. They have a greater gas mileage, produces less pollution and produces much more power and torque than just a mechanical engine. Jet planes, trains all the public transports is fast and safe now. Also, technology has changed the agricultural industry by replacing human labor with machines that are operated or controlled by people. Technology has helped farmers replace the old ways of farming with machines that can do the job in less time and effort. And the production rate has been increased hugely.



Negative Effects of Technology

On the other hand, the evolution of modern technology has disadvantages too. Technology has weakened the relations among family members. It has reduced the physical interaction between the people. People are attached to their screens almost 24/7. Studies have suggested that the lack of real life contact is causing depression and other forms of mental illness in many people. People are being more and more home staying than outgoing which adversely affects their mental health. Pornographic, violent, and other inappropriate materials can be accessed and viewed so easily. So, it can have negative impacts mainly in childrens. People are getting different health issues like hearing loss, vision problem, neck strain and many more. People don't need to do as much work as they use to do in past because many gadgets are out there are made for doing our daily works and reduce human efforts nowadays. As most technological discoveries aim to reduce human effort, it would imply that more work is done by machines. So, if it goes like this then in future everything could be robot controlled, job opportunity can drastically decrease. Digital crimes are increasing too. Stealing private informations, cheatings, different scams has increased because technology is accessible to all nowadays. Social bullying, inappropriate contents can cause depression to the people and recent studies has also proved it which can lead to different health and mental problems and even sucides.



Conclusion

We use technology, depend on technology in our daily life and our needs and demands for technology keep on rising. Technology has played a big role in many fields like health care, job creation, data management, improving quality of life and many more. Technology will keep on changing based on the demands of people and the market. Although technology has many advantages and has improved our life so much, the negative side of technology should not be underestimated as well.
